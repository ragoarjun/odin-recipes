Gulab Jamun

Description

Gulab Jamun is a soft, juicy dessert made from milk solids, deep-fried until golden, then soaked in a warm cardamom-flavored sugar syrup.

Each bite is sweet, rich, and melts in your mouth.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup milk powder
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour (maida)
  • 1/4 tsp baking soda
  • 2 tbsp ghee or melted butter
  • 1/4 cup milk (adjust as needed to make dough)
  • Oil or ghee for deep frying
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup water
  • 3-4 cardamom pods (crushed)

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix the milk powder, all-purpose flour, and baking soda.
  2. Add ghee and mix until the mixture turns crumbly.
  3. Gradually add milk and gently knead into a soft, smooth dough. Let it rest for 5-10 minutes.
  4. In a pan, combine sugar, water, and crushed cardamom pods. Boil until slightly sticky.
  5. Divide the dough into small, smooth balls without cracks.
  6. Heat oil or ghee on low to medium heat.
  7. Fry the balls slowly, stirring constantly, until golden brown.
  8. Remove and immediately place them into the warm sugar syrup.
  9. Let the Gulab Jamuns soak for at least 1-2 hours before serving.
