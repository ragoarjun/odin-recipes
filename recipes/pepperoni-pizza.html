Pepperoni Pizza

Description

Pepperoni pizza is a classic favorite featuring a crispy, chewy crust topped with tangy tomato sauce, gooey melted cheese, and a generous layer of spicy, savory pepperoni slices.

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup sliced pepperoni
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder (optional)
  • A pinch of red pepper flakes (optional for spice)

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface into your desired shape and thickness.
  3. Transfer the dough to a baking sheet or pizza stone.
  4. Spread a thin layer of tomato sauce evenly on the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  5. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese generously over the sauce.
  6. Arrange the pepperoni slices evenly on top of the cheese.
  7. Drizzle olive oil over the crust and sprinkle with dried oregano and garlic powder.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven for 10-12 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
  9. Remove from the oven, slice, and serve hot!
